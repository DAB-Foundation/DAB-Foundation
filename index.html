Two Reserve Funds

Assets

Four Assets

Deposit Token (DPT) is the shares you hold in Deposit Reserve Fund. Credit Token(CDT) is the shares you hold in Credit Reserve Fund and meanwhile stands for your credit line. SubCredit Token(SCT) is a form of Credit Token in the process of a loan and represents the condition in debt. Discredit Token(DCT) indicates your dishonesty in transactions.
